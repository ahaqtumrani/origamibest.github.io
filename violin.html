Take a Minuet to Look at this Amazing Music-Themed
Origami



You can fold pretty much anything you can imagine from paper and that includes things like
musical instruments, musicians and other music themed things.

There’s actually a pretty surprising amount of awesome music themed models and
we’ll be covering a lot of it in this post starting with
this paper bass. The entire thing is folded from one sheet of paper!
