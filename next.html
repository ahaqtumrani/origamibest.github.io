Hello! Welcome to AHTumrani.me

Our goal is to show more people how to make origami while helping them
improve their skills and promoting artists and others in the international paper
folding community. If you’ve never folded anything before want to learn
how, we have an excellent guide for beginners. We have a giant database
of free diagrams and video instrutions too. Our blog features amazing
work from a variety of very talented artists, we also have a collection of
book reviews and recommendations plus much more!
